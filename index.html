Brutal Trolls Attack Innocent Twitch Musician

Straight off the hot heels of Twitch streamer Ninja's racial slur scandal, there has occurred another big event surrounding the Twitch community. This time the EVE Online Goonswarn Federation, known for their greifing in the game, decided to raid a small streamer who goes by the name mozg888vadim, known as Vadim. He is far from the uber popular Fortnite specialist, Ninja, but instead hosts a polarizing small audience (usually around 10 people) and plays music on his accordion instead of trending games.

On Saturday, March. 24th, a member of Goonswarn found his way to Vadim's channel and invited his fellow members to join. Due to the surprise of gaining a proportionally big bump in viewers, Vadim decided to start taking requests. He instantly got one for playing "Little Bees" a popular parody of The Beatles' hit song "Let it Be".


Mozg888vadim's take on "Little Bees"


After Goonswarm had Vadin play this parody, which is known by almost all EVE players, the clip was posted to their subreddit and made Vadin's initial small audience of 10 people grow to a whopping 500 at the peak. Vadin had let his new audience know that he was streaming in order to collect tips so that he could buy his little sister a birthday present. Least to say, Vadin achieved his goal. By the end of the stream he had collected over $750 and the priceless support of even more loyal subscribers to his talent.

All-in-all it seems that this wholesome Twitch tale, which started as a troll-filled raid, turned out to be not bad at all. The internet has lately, and some would argue always, been surrounded by a thick fog of toxicity. It was refreshing to see a dent in that narrative. A break in the clouds that allowed just a ray of sunshine to fall in and warm all out hearts. Best of luck Vadin! And to all those who need it.
